# PRODUCT

- Audience: engineers evaluating or wiring RelativeDB into their own stack — people
  who already know SQL and their own schema, and want to know exactly what the
  engine does with their data before they trust a prediction.
- Register: docs
- Voice: precise, plain-spoken, fun
- Anti-references: AI-startup launch copy ("unlock", "supercharge", "10x"); vendor
  benchmark marketing; anything that describes a capability without saying what it
  costs or where it breaks.

## House notes

- **Second person, active voice.** "You declare the shape of your data", not "the
  shape of the data is declared".
